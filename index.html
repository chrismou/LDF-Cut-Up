Leeds Digital Festival is a celebration of the amazing talent this great city has to offer. Whether you're a digital ninja, underground urban artist, eager student, collaborator freelancer fresh with ideas, it's a chance for you to enjoy and explore the digital scene in all its glory.
The festival's not just for you, it's by you too. As a showcase of our collective talent, the festival is delivered entirely by the Leeds digital community so we need you and that talent of yours to help create the festival from the branding to the collateral, installation, events and exhibitions.
